Personal History

As a kid I loved to dismantle any technology I could get my hands on. This led to a large collection of various computers and hardware that I would never have any use for. I am happy to say that this has continued on into my adulthood and I still love to horde any technology I can find.

I always knew I wanted to work with computers, I absolutely loved the stimulation of having a problem and trying to solve it. To this day causing finding problems with tech and fixing them is one of my favorite things to do. Nothing is better than the tension I get when I work with a new technology, and the satisfaction it brings when I get it to work.
